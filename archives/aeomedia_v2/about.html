I am aeomanic, but aeomanic in not my name. I have 18 years of experience living, but I will never become expert in life. I like to simplify my life. Although ironic, everything I touch is complex. I enjoy relaxing, but I bombard myself with projects and goals. I am a hedonist, but I am wrong about this. Most of all, I enjoy closure, but often I procrastinate. Nonetheless, with all these uncertainties in my life, I am sure of one thing; I enjoy expressing my emotions and thoughts through graphic art.
